Sourdough Rye Bread

Delicious and easy to make rye bread. We make this every week.

Watch the video here

Ingredients

100g active sourdough starter

460ml water

150g rye kernels

100g linseeds

100g pumpkin seeds

100g sunflower seeds

1/2 Tbsp honey

100g flour

100g rye flour

1/2 Tbsp salt

Steps:


Day 1 Evening:

  1. Combine 100ml of active sourdough starter and 250ml of water.
  2. Add the following: 150g rye kernels, 100g linseeds, 100g pumpkin seeds, 100g sunflower seeds, 1/2 Tbsp honey
  3. Combine everything, cover with damp tea towel or plastic wrap, and leave in a warm place for 8 hours or overnight.

Day 2 Morning:

  1. Add the following to the sourdough and seed mixture: 210ml water, 100g flour, 100g rye flour, 1/2 Tbsp salt
  2. Combine everything, cover with damp tea towel or plastic wrap, and leave in a warm place for 2 hours.
  3. Pour bread dough into a non-stick baking pan.
  4. Cover with damp tea towel or plastic wrap and leave in a warm place for 2 hours.
  5. Bake in a pre-heated oven at 180C for approximately 1 hour.
