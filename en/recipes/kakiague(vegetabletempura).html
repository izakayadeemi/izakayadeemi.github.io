Kakiague (Vegetable Tempura)

Thinly sliced veggies coated in a crispy tempura batter - great as a side dish or appetizer! Enjoy them with a sprinkle of salt, dip them in tentsuyu (tempura sauce), or with both (my favorite)!

Watch the video here

Ingredients

2 carrots

1/2 yellow onion

2 scallions

4 big pearl oyster mushrooms

1 egg

220ml water

150g flour

30g cornstarch

1/8 tsp baking powder

frying oil

Steps:

  1. Julienne carrot and yellow onion. Tear pearl oyster mushrooms to bite size pieces and cut scallions so they are as long as the julienned carrot (around 7 cm).
  2. Prepare tempura batter by beating egg with water until mixture is foamy. Add flour, cornstarch, and baking powder.
  3. Prepare tentsuyu by combining dashi, mirin, and soy sauce. Boil for 1 minute and set aside.
  4. Put carrot, onion, pearl oyster mushrooms, and scallions into batter and deep fry until crispy on both sides, around 3 minutes on each side.
