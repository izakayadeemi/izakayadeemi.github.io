A Third Bread

Delicious and easy to make rye bread. We make this every week.

Ingredients

100g active sourdough starter

460ml water

150g rye kernels

100g linseeds

100g pumpkin seeds

100g sunflower seeds

1/2 Tbsp honey

100g flour

100g rye flour

1/2 Tbsp salt

0

Day 1 Evening:

1

Combine 100ml of active sourdough starter and 250ml of water.

2

Add the following: g rye kernels g rye kernels, g linseeds g rye kernels, g linseeds, g pumpkin seeds g rye kernels, g linseeds, g pumpkin seeds, g sunflower seeds g rye kernels, g linseeds, g pumpkin seeds, g sunflower seeds, Tbsp honey

3

Combine everything, cover with damp tea towel or plastic wrap, and leave in a warm place for 8 hours or overnight.

4

Day 2 Morning:

5

Add the following to the sourdough and seed mixture: ml water ml water, g flour ml water, g flour, g rye flour ml water, g flour, g rye flour, Tbsp salt

6

Combine everything, cover with damp tea towel or plastic wrap, and leave in a warm place for 2 hours.

7

Pour bread dough into a non-stick baking pan.

8

Cover with damp tea towel or plastic wrap and leave in a warm place for 2 hours.

9

Bake in a pre-heated oven at 180C for approximately 1 hour.
