Miso Cookies

This cookie has a brown butter base, a savoury hint from the miso, and a crunchy sesame bottom. As with all cookies, it's best if you wait until they cool down :)

Watch the video here

Ingredients

50g white sesame seeds

125g butter

80g granulated sugar

80g brown sugar

80g white miso

2 Tbs honey

3 teaspoons of vanilla extract

1 egg

188g flour

1/2 teaspoon baking soda

Steps:

  1. Roast white sesame seeds in a 170C pre-heated oven for 10 minutes.
  2. Brown the butter.
  3. Once butter is cooled down, add in the sugars and beat until dissolved.
  4. Stir in white miso, honey, vanilla extract, and egg.
  5. Fold in flour and baking soda.
  6. Shape cookies into hockey pucks and cover the bottom and sides with roasted sesame seeds.
