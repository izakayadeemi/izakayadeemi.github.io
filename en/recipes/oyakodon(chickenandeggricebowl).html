Oyakodon (Chicken and Egg Rice Bowl)

Tender chicken and eggs cooked in a savory sauce and served over warm rice - Japanese comfort food in 15 minutes!

Watch the video here

Ingredients

240g white rice

200ml dashi

2 Tbsp soy sauce

2 Tbsp sake

1 Tbsp mirin

1 tsp brown sugar

4 deboned chicken thighs

2/3 yellow onion

3 eggs

2 scallions

Steps:

  1. Wash and cook rice.
  2. Slice yellow onion into thin stripes, cut the green parts of scallions, and cut chicken thighs into bite-size pieces.
  3. In a skillet, combine dashi, soy sauce, sake, mirin, and brown sugar. Boil until sugar has dissolved.
  4. Layer onions evenly over sauce and cook until softened, around 2 minutes.
  5. Distribute chicken pieces evenly over onions and cook until chicken is no longer pink, around 5 minutes. Flip the chicken halfway. Sprinkle half of the scallions over cooked chicken.
  6. Beat eggs and pour oven chicken.
